"I swear to you, here and now: I, Hildibrand, agent of enquiry, inspector extraordinaire, shall scour all creation─from the deepest pit of the seven hells to the very pinnacle of the heavens─for your Pure Heart!"

Timeline

1549 - Born in Eorzea, Ul’dah and raised by his parents Godbert Manderville and Julyan.
1572 - Grew up to become an "agent of enquiry, inspector extraordinaire" with his loyal assistant Nashu Mhakaracca.
1572 - Attempted to stop a primal summoning, and was sent up into the sky by Titan going missing for years and presumed dead but miraculously survived.
1577 - Due to his long absence and unusual behavior, many mistook him for an undead zombie and he then met the Warrior of Light.
1577 - Collaborating with the Warrior of Light, Hildibrand crossed paths with Gilgamesh, the famous weapon-collector, leading to an over-the-top duel.
1577-1578 - After defeating Gilgamesh, Hildibrand started the search for the mysterious Phantom Thief failing many times to catch them but in the end solved the case and stopped them.
1578 - After traversing Ishgardian territories, he once again found himself being flung into the sky, this time in a homemade aether-powered rocket.
1579 - Instead of landing back in Eorzea, he landed in Kugane where he was mistaken for a statue and stolen by a burglar putting him through another adventure.
1582 - After his adventure in Kugane, he disappeared once again but this time in The First where he was mistaken for an enemy and punched into a wall before being plucked from the wall by a sin eater and soon believed that he was a sin eater and the Warrior of Light helped him come to his senses.
1582-1583 - After his adventure in The First, he was immediately abducted by aliens taking him on another ridiculous journey into Thavnair.
1583 - After finishing his adventure in Thavnair the UFO that was used to kidnap him turned on randomly as he was inspecting it and sent him flying off once again.
1584 - His whereabouts now are unknown but we’ve been lead to the city of Solution Nine.
